What is ICT?

ICT stands for Information and Communication Technology. It refers to technologies that provide access to information and communication. It includes everything from computers and the internet to phones and cloud services.

Main Components of ICT

  • Networks: LAN, WAN, internet
  • Hardware: Computers, smartphones, routers
  • Software: Apps, operating systems, web tools
  • Communication: Email, video calls, messaging
  • Data: Storage, processing, cybersecurity
