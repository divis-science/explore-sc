What is Biology?

Biology is the study of life and living organisms. It explores how life works, how organisms grow, evolve, interact, and survive. Biology is essential for medicine, agriculture, conservation, and understanding the environment.

Major Branches of Biology

  • Cell Biology – Study of cells and their functions
  • Genetics – Study of heredity and DNA
  • Ecology – Study of how organisms interact with their environment
  • Evolution – Study of how life changes over time
  • Human Biology – Study of human body systems
  • Microbiology – Study of bacteria, viruses, and other microbes

Famous Discoveries

  • Discovery of DNA structure by Watson and Crick
  • Darwin’s Theory of Evolution
  • Pasteur’s Germ Theory

Why Biology Matters

Biology helps us cure diseases, protect the environment, grow food, and understand ourselves. From medicine to nature conservation, biology is everywhere.
